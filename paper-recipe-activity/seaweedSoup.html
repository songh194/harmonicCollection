Seaweed Soup (2 servings)

Ingredients

minced garlic, sesamee oil, dried seaweed, beef (optional), soy sauce

Steps

1.Get a bowl and put 2 Tbsp of dried seaweed. Also, heat the pot up and add sesamee oil to it.
2. Drain the water and add the seaweed to the pot
3. Add 1 Tbsp of minced garlic and beef to the pot.
4. Cook til the meat is done.
5. Add 2 cups of water and season it with soy sauce.
6. done!
